Types of Cyber Attacks

Phishing

Phishing is a method where attackers trick users into providing sensitive information such as passwords or bank details.

Malware

Malware includes viruses, worms, and spyware that infiltrate systems to steal data or damage devices.

Ransomware

Ransomware encrypts user data and demands payment for restoring access. One of the most dangerous attacks.

Denial of Service (DoS)

DoS attacks overload servers, making websites and services unavailable to users.
