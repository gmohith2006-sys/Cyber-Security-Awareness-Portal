Cyber Safety Tips

🔒 Use Strong Passwords

Create long, complex passwords with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols.

🔑 Enable Two-Factor Authentication

Always enable 2FA for extra security when logging in.

⚠ Beware of Suspicious Links

Never click on unknown links or email attachments.

🛡 Keep Software Updated

Regular updates fix security bugs and protect you from new threats.

🌐 Browse Safely

Use secure HTTPS websites and avoid unsafe public Wi-Fi.
